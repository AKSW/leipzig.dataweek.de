Data Week Leipzig 2022

Quo Vadis Informatik in the Leipzig/Halle Metropolitan Area

2022-07-08

The members of the Leipzig/Halle region group of the Gesellschaft für Informatik e.V. and the German Chapter of the ACM (stakeholders from science, business, politics) will meet to plan activities to promote computer science in the region. The meeting is for networking and developing ideas and is open to all interested persons.

The Gesellschaft für Informatik e.V. is a non-profit association of committed computer scientists in the German-speaking region with around 20,000 personal members. The Gesellschaft für Informatik e.V. offers its members a professional network for the exchange of knowledge and experience on all current and relevant topics in computer science. The members are organized in 14 sections within the field of computer science organized in 150 expert groups and more than 30 regional groups. https://gi.de
