Data Week Leipzig 2022

10th Leipzig Semantic Web Day

Knowledge Graphs for Organizational Data Spaces
2022-07-05

Since 2009, the Leipzig Semantic Web Day (LSWT) is an opportunity for scientists, enterprises, and organisations to discuss topics in the field of Semantic Technology. These emphases are demonstrated in presentations by experts from industry and science. If you have any further questions don’t hesitate to contact the organisation team.

Call for Contributions

Download Call

During the LSWT we will emphasize recent developments and trends in the fields of semantic technologies and data. Main points this year shall be knowledge graphs and linked data, data integration, semantic web in industry, government and society, as well as artificial intelligence. These emphases are demonstrated in presentations by experts from industry and science. We invite you to present your current work and latest ideas in a 15 - 30 minutes presentation. Contributions considering women in the mentioned fields are more than warmly welcomed. Presentations shall be held in English.

Beside sessions and keynotes, we are going to host a poster session with student works. Therefore, please feel free to share this call with any student who might be interested.

We look forward to your contributions to make this event a success! Please send your ideas for a presentation until 1 June 2022 to Natanael Arndt: natanael.arndt@eccenca.com. If you have any further questions don’t hesitate to contact us.
