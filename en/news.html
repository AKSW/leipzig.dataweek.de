News

  • Data Week Leipzig - June 26th to June 30th
    2023-03-30

    Mark your calendars for Data Week Leipzig 2023 and join us from June 26th to June 30th, for a week of learning, networking and innovation. Discover scientific experts' latest trends and advancements in data and AI and take part in interactive workshops and trainings, hear inspiring keynote speeches and connect with fellow professionals. Data Week Leipzig unites individual event formats in the field of research and transfer, which are organized by the participating scientific and transfer institutions Institute for Applied Informatics. and ScaDS.AI Dresden/ Leipzig, as well as eccenca GmbH, the DBpedia Association, and the AKSW network. This year's program is already in development and will soon be published on our new website.

  • New website online!
    2023-02-01

    We are very pleased to introduce our new Data Week website just in time for Data Week Leipzig 2023! Following on from the successful conference week in 2022, the network platform that has been created is now being expanded and developed. Discover the latest information on keynote speakers, workshops, and networking events and start planning your Data Week Leipzig 2023 experience today.
