  • Recap: Data Week Leipzig 2024
    2024-05-03

    Over 530 participants networked at this year’s Data Week and exchanged ideas on the topics of artificial intelligence, digitalisation and data at Leipzig’s New Town Hall in mid-April. Among the more than 150 programme items were a total of 26 workshops, 10 training sessions and 77 lectures, in which participants had the opportunity to develop their own skills and learn new things about data management, urban development, AI tools, crisis resilience and digital rights. Our colleagues from the Institute of Applied Informatics have published a detailed recap of the entire week of events. Check more details here.

  • Data Week Leipzig 2024 - program online
    2024-01-25

    The contents of the various tracks and workshops are now online on our website and are continuously updated. Discover our diverse program on the topics of KI, semantic technologies and the potential of digitalisation and data use in various fields of work. You can now see all the planned sessions taking place during Data Week Leipzig under the menu item “Tracks”. In addition to a KMI transfer day, there will be exciting workshops on the topics of GPT, digitalisation in education, sustainable AI and 3D printing platforms, as well as training sessions and tutorials that provide an insight into use cases and data sets.

  • Data Week Leipzig 2024 - April 15th to 19th
    2023-09-28

    Prepare for the upcoming third edition of Data Week Leipzig, scheduled to take place from April 15th to 19th, 2024. This event promises another week of learning, networking and innovation in the field of data and AI. You’ll have the opportunity to explore the latest trends and advancements, attend interactive workshops, hear inspiring keynotes and connect with fellow professionals. Data Week Leipzig is jointly organized by the Institute for Applied Informatics, ScaDS.AI Dresden/ Leipzig and eccenca GmbH. The program for next year is currently under development and will be available soon on our website. Stay tuned for further updates!
