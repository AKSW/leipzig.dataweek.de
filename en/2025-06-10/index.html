Side Events

13:00

Roadshow: Build your financial capacity Smart and climate neutral - Digital Twins for energy transition and climate neutrality

13:00 - 16:00 | Untere Wandelhalle | English


The Roadshow is a service offered by the Scalable Cities Secretariat to support cities in the field of financial design of projects such as business model, financial schemes and everything that unfolds from it. With this objective, a series of activities will be carried out through which basic and advanced skills on finance will be acquired, to have resources with which to think and design economically sustainable and scalable urban projects to achieve climate neutrality. Get more information: https://smart-cities-marketplace.ec.europa.eu/scalable-cities/roadshow-build-your-financial-capacity

16:10

Leipzig, how are you? - A walk through sensor technology and user-generated data

16:10 - 17:40 | Smart City Lab | German


Sensors are increasingly appearing in Leipzig’s cityscape and are needed as a technology for ‘measuring’ urban tasks. In addition to sensors, citizen and user-generated data is an as yet untapped treasure for sustainable urban development. On our walk through Leipzig’s city centre, you will discover where and how sensors and data donations are used in the city and how they are already making our city smarter today (start: New City Hall, destination: Smart City Lab, approx. 3,5km)

17:45

Visit city hall tower

17:45 - 18:15 | | German


Built on the foundation of the old Pleißenburg Tower, the tower of the New City Hall is considered the tallest city hall tower in Germany and is one of Leipzig’s most important landmarks. With a height of exactly 114.7 meters, it towers over the entire city. As part of Data Week Leipzig, you have the opportunity to visit the town hall tower with us.
