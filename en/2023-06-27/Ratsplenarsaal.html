14:00

Hackathon Winners Award Ceremony

14:00 - 15:30 | Ratsplenarsaal


True to the motto “Free Data!”, the “Leipzig Open Data Hackathon” is taking place this year. Here, innovative ideas are to be developed to bring urban data to the people faster and more effectively. Interested citizens, students, IT enthusiasts and open data enthusiasts are invited to make the existing offering of the Leipzig Open Data portal even better with their own ideas and suggestions. In this slot, the prize winners will be awarded.

  • Leipzig Winners
  • FINEST Winner 1
  • FINEST Winner 2

15:10

Session Data competence

15:10 - 16:30 | Ratsplenarsaal


Social data literacy should not be left to chance. Data literacy should therefore not only be a skill of future generations, but must already become a basic building block for the education of people of different ages. In this session, representatives from science, industry and administration will present innovative projects that are currently being implemented in and around Leipzig on data literacy.
