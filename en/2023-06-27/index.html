Tuesday

Subscribt to day calendar

While continuing the discussions around dataspaces in various domains, the Urban Planning Day is dedicated to focusing on the challenges facing cities as they adapt to climate change. The day is themed "Free Data! Skills and Tools for Climate-Adapted Cities". The Connected Urban Twins project and other city projects will show how data from different data sources can be combined and how, among other things, the use of digital twins can provide important insights for urban development. In addition, various players from research, administration and business will present their solutions and initiatives for shaping sustainable and climate-friendly urban development. For example, sessions, training and workshops will discuss digital building permits, urban platforms, or generally the challenge of how to make environmental data Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Re-usable (FAIR) and improve the tools needed for planning and reporting.

Visit city hall tower

Built on the foundation of the old Pleißenburg Tower, the tower of the New City Hall is considered the tallest city hall tower in Germany and is one of Leipzig’s most important landmarks. With a height of exactly 114.7 meters, it towers over the entire city. As part of Data Week Leipzig, you have the opportunity to visit the town hall tower with us.
