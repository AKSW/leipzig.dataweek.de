13:00

Hackathon Winners Award Ceremony

13:00 - 14:30 | Ratsplenarsaal


True to the motto “Free Data!”, the “Leipzig Open Data Hackathon” is taking place this year. Here, innovative ideas are to be developed to bring urban data to the people faster and more effectively. Interested citizens, students, IT enthusiasts and open data enthusiasts are invited to make the existing offering of the Leipzig Open Data portal even better with their own ideas and suggestions. In this slot, the prize winners will be awarded.
