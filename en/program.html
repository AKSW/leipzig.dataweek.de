  • Monday

    GDDS
     ScaDS

    OGC hosts the day's program under the motto “EU Green Deal Data Space” in the Sitzungssaal and the Festsaal. In parallel, Scads.AI offers training on data visualization in the Council Plenary Room. The workshop “Green Deal Data Spaces” will take place in room 259.

  • Tuesday

    GDDS
     Free!
     LODH

    The 2nd conference day is themed “Free data! Competencies and Tools for Climate-Adapted Cities”. This will open with an exciting keynote presentation and awards ceremony. The conference program will take place in parallel in the Sitzungssaal and Ratsplenarsaal (council plenary room), with the “Geospatial Ecosystem” workshop being held in room 259.

  • Wednesday

    GDDS
     ScaDS
     DSM
     KMI
     LSWT

    For the events Leipzig Semantic Web Day (Sitzungssaal), Data Science Mania (Festsaal) and the KMI Consortium Meeting (Festsaal/council plenary room), various stakeholders will come together on Day 3. Scads.AI training on the Python programming language for City of Leipzig staff will be held in room 260. The OGC Semantic Workshop will be held in room 259.

  • Thursday

    ScaDS
     AI-T

    The working conference on “Artificial Intelligence Development for a Resilient and Sustainable Tomorrow” (AI Tomorrow) will bring together researchers in the Ratsplenarsaal (council plenary room) to discuss the environmental, social and economic implications of the design, development and deployment of AI in Industry 5.0. In parallel, the Scads.AI training “Fundamentals Machine Learning with Python” will take place in room 259.
