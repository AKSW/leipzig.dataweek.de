Call for Event Submission

    Since 2022, Data Week Leipzig has been a networking and exchange event that explores the scientific, economic, and social perspectives of data and its utilization. During Data Week Leipzig 2025, workshops, training sessions, and hackathons can be organized. The conference week will take place from May 5 to 9, 2025, at Leipzig’s New City Hall.

    This year’s event, approximately 500 representatives from administration, research, and industry gathered at the New City Hall Leipzig over five days to exchange ideas about ongoing and future projects. Furthermore, Data Week Leipzig provides an excellent platform for networking, sharing experiences, and recruiting

    We particularly invite event proposals that address interdisciplinary and cross-regional topics related to data, digitalization, and artificial intelligence.

    Workshops can be submitted on the following topics:

    • Data Science (Big Data, Data Analytics, Semantics)
    • Open Data Lifecycle – From Data Acquisition to Application
    • Digitalization and Urban Development (Mobility, Energy, Smart Cities)
    • AI Challenges in Administration, Science, and Industry (e.g., Application of LLM, Development and Utilization of Digital Twins)
    • Hardware Development for AI Systems (e.g., IoT, Sensor Technology for Data Sources)
    • Digital Tools for Citizen Participation
    • Future of the Healthcare Sector
    • Sustainability and New Technologies
    • Leadership in Data/Digitalization/AI Teams
    • Project Management in Data/Digitalization and AI Projects
    • Ethical Considerations in Data/Digitalization and AI Projects

    Furthermore, training sessions with the following focuses can be conducted:

    • Data Science Applications
    • Open Data – Metadata Design and Provision
    • Data Visualization, Data Analysis
    • Artificial Intelligence, Knowledge Graphs
    • Introduction to Data-Driven Technologies, Methods, Tools, and Trends
    • Hardware Implementation (e.g., IoT, Sensor Technology for Data Sources)
    • Leadership and Organizational Structure in Data/Digitalization/AI Teams
    • Project Management in Data/Digitalization/AI Projects

    IT companies are invited to present their own products, datasets, or use cases.

    Submissions for hackathons are also welcome. Last year, the Leipzig Open Data Hackathon was organized by the Office of Statistics and Elections of the City of Leipzig. We are open to further and different topic suggestions. Please get in touch with us as soon as possible.

    Submission

    Submissions for all Data Week Leipzig sub-events are now open and can be made until November 15, 2024, via dataweek[at]infai.org. Submissions should include a brief summary of the event (abstract with a maximum of 500 words) and a description of how a sufficient number of participants will be acquired or addressed. Please provide the name(s) and email address(es) of the contact person(s), event format, and duration (half-day, full-day) when registering. If there is an existing community or previous events, feel free to include links in the PDF document. Please send us your abstract as a PDF file and specify whether the presentation language is German or English.

    We expect the organizers of sub-events to personally participate in Data Week Leipzig and register for the conference themselves.

    Submissions will be processed based on the date of receipt: We aim to encourage active participation from all participating organizations to expand the overall reach of Data Week Leipzig. Therefore, decisions will be made on a ‘first come, first served’ basis. The review criteria are set as follows: 1) The theme of the sub-event must align with the topics of Data Week, 2) The explanation of the sub-event’s ability to attract a sufficient number of participants must be clear and convincing.”

    Schedule

    • Deadline for Abstract Submissions: November 15, 2024
    • Announcement of All Event Formats: November 30, 2024
    • Submission of All Program and Session Information (Agenda, etc.): January 31, 2025
    • Final Program Online: March 1, 2025
    • Event Week: May 5th, 2025 - May 9th, 2025

    For inquiries, please contact us at dataweek[at]infai.org.

    We look forward to your contribution!
