Data Week Leipzig 2022

DBpedia PhD Symposium

2022-07-06

The aim of the symposium is to provide an opportunity to PhD students to present their PhD work-in-progress and receive feedback on their work. In an open, supportive and non-confrontational environment the students will present their PhD research topic in relation to the state-of-the-art, the identified gaps, addressed challenges, considered methods for exploitation, datasets for validation, and the overall evaluation approach. The symposium will attend senior academics, representatives from companies and organizations from the DBpedia Community Board and other PhD student candidates. During the discussion part the attendees will provide feedback and general recommendations for improvement. The DBpedia PhD symposium will help PhD students to strengthen and shape their work as they progress towards their PhD degree. Moreover, the symposium is a great opportunity to build a network of peers for the future PhD career. The overall objective of the symposium is to promote excellence in PhD research in the knowledge and information engineering field.

The Format

The symposium will be organized as an on-site event. It will be organized as an open event where anyone can register and attend. Each PhD presenter will receive a 30 minutes slot which includes 20 minutes for presentation of the research topic and 10 minutes for questions, feedback and discussion.
