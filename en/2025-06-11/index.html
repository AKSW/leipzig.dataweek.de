Side Events

15:30

The art of data: AI compression and decompression of ERA5 global climate data (starts at 4pm)

15:30 - 17:00 | | German


AI offers great potential for increasing efficiency in the processing of weather data. In this event at the interface of science, art and entertainment, participants will experience the fascinating possibilities of AI-supported compression and decompression using the example of ERA5 world climate data. Be inspired by how art and technology come together to make AI and complex data visible and tangible. (Start from 16:00)

17:10

Visit city hall tower

17:10 - 17:40 | | German


Built on the foundation of the old Pleißenburg Tower, the tower of the New City Hall is considered the tallest city hall tower in Germany and is one of Leipzig’s most important landmarks. With a height of exactly 114.7 meters, it towers over the entire city. As part of Data Week Leipzig, you have the opportunity to visit the town hall tower with us.

19:00

ScaDS.AI Meetup @ Wandelhalle: Networking, exchange & open discussion

19:00 - 20:15 | Wandelhalle | German


Take the opportunity to exchange ideas with the speakers and other participants in a relaxed atmosphere. Whether spontaneous discussions, specific questions or new ideas - there is room for networking and inspiration. The following contributions will be shown in the demo session:

  • “See one, do one, teach one” is the motto of Pamemo. The AI-supported learning app enables practical and interactive education on a smartphone from the comfort of your own home. It is currently being used in Leipzig and Africa to train doctors - primarily on the topics of resuscitation and antibiotic therapy. Contact persons are Adrian Viehweger and Sebastian Driesnack (Leipzig Medical Faculty).
  • “alphaspeech voicebot” - Customizable voicebot framework for data-secure voice interaction. alphaspeech voicebot was developed for applications where data protection, flexibility and domain-specific adaptability are paramount. Voicebots can be optimally adapted to the respective language domain, the application context and specific usage requirements. Your contact person is Felix Gräßer (alpha NT GmbH).
