Data Science Mania

At Data Science Mania, we will talk about leadership and agility in the data science environment, about successful and perhaps not so successful projects (and what you can learn from them) and about promising methodological approaches - regardless of the programming language and framework. All people who are interested in the topics are invited, regardless of their professional background and career level. After all, that’s what Data Science is really about: different people inspiring each other and creating something new.
