14th Leipzig Semantic Web Day

Since 2009, the Leipzig Semantic Web Day (LSWT) is an opportunity for scientists, enterprises, and organisations to discuss topics in the field of Semantic Technology. The 14th Leipzig Semantic Web Day (#LSWT2026) will once again be held in conjunction with the Data Week Leipzig on June 3rd, 2026, taking place in the heart of Leipzig. During LSWT2026, we will highlight the latest developments and trends in semantic technologies and data. The focus topics for this year include:

  • Current challenges in the application of Large Language Models
  • LLM and Knowledge Graph integration
  • Semantic technologies and AI for industry and science
  • New data products for industrial automation
  • Cybersecurity and Knowledge Graphs
  • Secure enterprise knowledge management
  • Knowledge Graph use cases and sustainability
  • FAIR data and data catalogs
  • Semantic Data Fabric for real-world use cases
  • Networked Society

Please mind that the LWST will be held in English.

If you have any further questions don’t hesitate to contact the organisation team: lswt@eccenca.com.

Call for Participation

Download Call

If you are interested in participating, we invite you to present your current work in a 15-minute talk. Presentations will be held in English. We particularly welcome contributions from women in the field of Data Science. We look forward to your contributions to make this event a success! Please send your ideas for a presentation until January 31, 2026 to lswt@eccenca.com.

If you have any further questions, don’t hesitate to contact us. We are looking forward to meeting you in Leipzig.
