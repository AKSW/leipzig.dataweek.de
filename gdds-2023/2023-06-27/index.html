17:00

Training: Location Innovation Academy

17:00 - 18:20 | Ratsplenarsaal | Englisch

Alpo Turunen (Nationale Landvermessung) und Anniina Lundvall (Nationale Landvermessung)

Do you want to learn new technologies helping to achieve a sustainable and resilient future? Become a master of location data and service management & integration with us! Location Innovation Academy provides free courses for beginners and professionals who want to learn about modern data management, service management and integration of services and data with the help of OGC API’s. In this session you will learn about our courses and explore effective ways to learn within the Academy. We discuss and share ideas, and as a result you will have a study plan for yourself. Take a computer with you for this training.
